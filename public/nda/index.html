1.0Definition of Confidential Information

"Confidential Information" means any non-public information disclosed by either Party, whether written, oral, or electronic, designated as confidential or reasonably understood to be so. 2.0Obligations of the Receiving Party

Each Party agrees to: (a) hold Confidential Information in strict confidence; (b) not disclose it to third parties without prior written consent; (c) use it solely for the collaboration defined herein; and (d) protect it with the same care as its own confidential information.

3.0Exclusions

Obligations do not apply to information that: (a) is publicly known through no act of the Receiving Party; (b) was rightfully known prior to disclosure; (c) is rightfully obtained from a third party; or (d) is independently developed without reference to the Confidential Information.
